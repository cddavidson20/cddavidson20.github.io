Background

Our Client, Taras, buys used cars, breaks them down, and lists car parts onto eBay.

We were tasked with creating a way to improve the way he lists these parts, as well as track various analytics regarding his postings.

Scope

We decided to create a web application in order to improve the selling experience, help our client with tracking these parts.

This project will greatly improve our clients efficency and allow him to address more important bussiness needs, while quickly seeing whatever data he wishes

Solution

Our solution is a web-based part management system that allows our client to easily track what parts he posts to eBay and what parts he has available

Our application allows the client to catalog listings, track analytics, and manage inventory all from one application.

Highlights

Our project is taylored to the needs of our consumer. Our client can manage his parts and track statistics realated to these parts.

Our application automates this inventory tracking proccess. Our client is able to pull up this application anywhere and quickly see what parts are available vs listed.

Technologies

We used ReactJS paired with a Flask backend and a MySQL database among with a lot of other small helpful packages. For our repository, we decided to use GitLab managing our code.
